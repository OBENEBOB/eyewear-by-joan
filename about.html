Our Story

EyewearByJoan was born from a simple realization: Cameroonians deserve access to quality eyewear that protects their eyes without compromising on style. Founded in 2020 from Douala, Bonabéri, what started as a passion project quickly grew into a trusted destination for premium photochromic and blue light glasses.

📍 Based in Douala, Bonabéri — serving customers nationwide.

We operate strictly online with pickup available in Douala, making quality eyewear accessible to everyone in Cameroon.

The journey hasn't always been easy—from struggling with supplier delays to building trust with customers who had never tried blue light glasses before. But every smile, every "thank you," and every repeat customer reminds us why we do this. Today, we've served thousands of happy customers across Cameroon and continue to grow.

Our Mission

To provide Cameroonians with high-quality, affordable eyewear that protects their eyes from digital strain and harmful UV rays while making them look and feel confident. We're committed to educating our community about eye health and making premium eye protection accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

To become Cameroon's most trusted eyewear brand—known for quality, style, and genuine care for our customers' eye health. We envision a future where every Cameroonian understands the importance of eye protection and has access to stylish, affordable eyewear.

What We Stand For

Genuine Care
